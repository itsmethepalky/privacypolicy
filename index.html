Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

QRocket does not collect or store your personal data. Your privacy is respected by design.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

  • Camera Access: Used only for scanning QR codes and barcodes. All processing happens on your device.
  • Local Storage: Scan history is stored only on your device and never transmitted.
  • Advertising Data: If ads are enabled, third-party services like Google AdMob may collect device-related identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide QR scanning and generation features
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display ads (if enabled)

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services to enhance functionality:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google ML Kit (QR scanning technology)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures. However, no system is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 and does not knowingly collect children's data.

7. Permissions

  • Camera: Required for scanning QR codes
  • Internet: Used for ads and updates

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page.
